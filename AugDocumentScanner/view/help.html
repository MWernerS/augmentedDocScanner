Augmented Document Scanner Help Page

Learn how to digitize your documents with ADS

  1. Upload photos of your document to be scanned
  2. Use the Upload Files button to upload images of your Augmented Documents to be scanned.
  3. Once your images are uploaded, press the Create PDF button and watch the magic happen.
  4. Use your new .PDF however you see fit.
